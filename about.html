Hello!
My name is Jan ('yawn'), and I make interactive experiences like websites, apps & interfaces, services, and games.
I always strive to make my experiences immersive, memorable, and delightful, through the use of research, user-centered design and lots of iterative prototyping.

I am the Senior User Experience Architect at Nurun, but am available for freelance.

I live in Atlanta, but I share citizenship between the United States and Sweden.

Work

I have been on both the agency and the client-side, and I've been lucky to have worked with some great companies.

I currently work for an interactive agency called Nurun, where I work with Nespresso, GE Energy, The Home Depot among others. My previous clients include Coca Cola, L'Oreal, Verizon, and Cartoon Network. I have also worked at places like Microsoft, and Lockheed Martin.
