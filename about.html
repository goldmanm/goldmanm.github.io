Experience

I have been on both the agency and the client-side, and I've been lucky to have worked with some great companies and clients.

I currently work for an interactive agency called Nurun, as a Senior User Experience Architect, where I work with Nespresso, GE Energy, The Home Depot among others.

Previously, I have worked with Coca Cola, L'Oreal, Verizon, and Cartoon Network. I have also worked at places like Microsoft, and Lockheed Martin.

I have a Masters of Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon, and have undergraduate degrees in Psychology and in Computer Science, focusing on game development, from UMBC.

More About Myself

I've always had a fascination with learning, doing, and experimenting, and my experience spans across research, design, and writing code.

I have experience with Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, Axure, and other design and productivity tools. I prefer to prototype websites in HTML, JavaScript and SASS/CSS, and other experiences in Flash, but I'm familiar with anything from C to Processing.

My eclectic background mirrors my eclectic upbringing. Though born in China, I moved to Stockholm at a young age.

I am fluent in English and Swedish, and conversational in Mandarin.

I hold dual citizenship with the United States and Sweden.

On my off days, I enjoy swing dancing, rock climbing, playing indie games, and enjoying good food.
