About this Site

This site's probably my umpteenth portfolio redesign, as I seem to want to create a new one every three months. This one will hopefully last longer, as it has a blog element, is written in SASS, is fairly robust, and is hosted for free at Github.

Initially I crawled through many CMS and hosting systems, including WordPress, and didn't like any of them: WordPress was too cumbersome for my needs, and the other sites were too costly on a long-term basis. I landed on Github Pages out of chance, found out you could host and blog for free, and are given complete freedom in design, thanks to a static generator technology called Jekyll.

At some point I'll create a post to show exactly how I created this site. For now, branch my repo and shoot me an e-mail for questions if you have any questions.

Icons are sourced from http://iconmonstr.com/
